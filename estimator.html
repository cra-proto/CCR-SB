How much you may get

Estimate your payment

Use this tool to estimate your amount based on your business operations between 2019 and 2024. You can also refer to the payment rate tables to manually calculate your payment.

You must be eligible to receive the rebate before you can complete the estimator.

Read the privacy statement for the estimator

The CRA is not collecting or retaining any of the information you enter in this tool. These amounts are only used to help you estimate the Canada Carbon Rebate payment amount you may be eligible for.

Canada Carbon Rebate for Small Businesses estimator

Eligible years to claim

Your rebate is a single amount that includes all past years you were eligible for.

The dates are based on a fuel charge year, which corresponds with the year date you filed your corporate tax return.

What is a fuel charge year?

Definition goes here

Year(s) you may be eligible for (required)
Employee residence

An employee’s residence is the province or territory their T4 was issued for that year (box 10).

Provinces or territories you have employees in during these periods (required)
Number of employees

If you issued more than one T4 for an employee in different provinces in the same year, include them only once, in province which their remuneration (box 14) was highest.

Amount of employees exceed eligibility

You have calculated the follow periods as having 500 employes or more across Canada:

  • 2023 fuel year
  • 2022 fuel year
  • 2021 fuel year
  • 2020 fuel year
  • 2019 fuel year

Therefore you are not eligible for the rebate for this period.
